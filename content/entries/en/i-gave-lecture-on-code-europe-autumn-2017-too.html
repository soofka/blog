How to setup routing for "Not Found" on both sides with React-Router and Express

Yup, I did it again. Second big lecture in my life took place, similarly to my debut, on Code Europe in Warsaw, Poland, this time during Autumn 2017 edition. On the afternoon of 7th of December I talked about my experience with building Progressive Web Apps, particularly in React. It was a case study based on two examples: big, complex application built for hundreds of thousands of users of StepStone; and small, home-brewed one: this blog.

During the lecture, I talked about Progressive Web App Checklist by Google and how to fulfill certain points, focusing mainly on Service Workers, Push Notifications, and how to implement them, based on real scenarios. (Hence "case study" in the title. In other words, it's one of those "a lot of code on the screen" lectures.)

Admittedly, it was a worse performance than my previous attempt, or at least I felt less prepared and in worse shape. I am glad for this experience, it gave me a few important lessons, one of them resulting in my decision that next lecture I will give, I will give only because I want to share something cool with people, not because I want to give a lecture. I want them to be inspiring, and for it to happen, I need to feel that way.
