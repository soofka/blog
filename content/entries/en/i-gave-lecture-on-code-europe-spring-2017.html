I gave a lecture on Code Europe Spring 2017!

That's right. Even before I became famous as a blog writer, I got an opportunity to give a lecture on one of the biggest IT conferences in Poland: Code Europe, Spring edition of year 2017. It was my first time as a speaker in front of crowd of that size and volume, so after a few weeks of panicky preparations, I managed to deliver presentation on how to optimize your JavaScript code with variety of tricks and techniques.

Apparently, my reception wasn’t that bad after all, since I was invited to give another lecture on this conference’s next edition, which will take place on Polish National Stadium in Warsaw on 7th of December. (I start panicking already.)

Slides, that I used to help me to get my point through, can be downloaded from here. In case of any other questions or suggestions related to what I was talking about when standing on that stage (or any other, really), feel free to contact me on Twitter.
